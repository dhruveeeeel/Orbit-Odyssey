Space Missions on Earth

NASA Missions

NASA has conducted numerous space missions exploring Earth and beyond. Some notable missions include:

  • Apollo Program: Sent astronauts to the Moon in the 1960s and 1970s.
  • Hubble Space Telescope: Launched in 1990, providing stunning images of distant galaxies.
  • Mars Rover Missions: Sent rovers like Curiosity to explore the Martian surface.
  • Earth Observing System (EOS): Series of satellites monitoring Earth's climate and ecosystems.

International Space Missions

Collaborative efforts between space agencies worldwide have led to remarkable achievements:

  • European Space Agency (ESA): Conducted missions like Rosetta, exploring comets.
  • Roscosmos (Russia): Participated in the International Space Station (ISS) and lunar exploration.
  • China National Space Administration (CNSA): Launched missions like Chang'e for lunar exploration.
